Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority

Last updated: December 9, 2024

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Try our courses risk-free. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your money.

Our Commitment

At CodeMaster Academy, we're confident in the quality of our courses. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all course purchases. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can request a full refund within 30 days of your purchase date.

No questions asked. We want you to be happy with your learning experience.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for a refund, your request must meet the following conditions:

  • The refund request is made within 30 days of the original purchase date
  • You have completed less than 30% of the course content
  • You have not previously received a refund for the same course
  • The course was not purchased during a special promotion with different terms

Note: Refunds are not available for courses that have been substantially completed (more than 30% progress) or if you've downloaded course materials.

Processing Time

Once your refund request is approved:

  1. Approval: We'll review and respond to your request within 2 business days
  2. Processing: Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  3. Bank Processing: Your bank or payment provider may take an additional 3-5 business days to credit your account

You'll receive an email confirmation once your refund has been processed.

Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Courses purchased more than 30 days ago
  • Courses with more than 30% completion
  • Subscription renewals (must be cancelled before renewal date)
  • Gift purchases redeemed by the recipient
  • Courses purchased with promotional credits or vouchers
